Accessibility

Pappy's Jr Accessibility Statement

Last updated December, 2023

Pappy's Jr is committed to providing accessibility at our restaurant location as well as on our website. Enabling us to serve the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and, in doing so, adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.

Accessibility at Pappy's Jr Restaurant

Pappy's Jr's diner location is wheelchair accessible and has handicap parking and bathrooms available. We provide service to everyone, regardless of their ability or situation. If you believe that you may need additional accomodations, please feel free to call us at (843)-764-7244 prior to your arrival and we would be happy to ensure your needs for are met for your dining experience with us.

Accessibility on pappyjr.com

Pappy's Jr strives to meet and improve on its compliance with ADA and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1). If you have any issues with using our website due to a disability or issues with assistance devices (e.g. screen readers) please let us know at pappysjr420@yahoo.com

Disclaimer

Pappy's Jr continues its efforts to constantly improve the accessibility of its site and services in the belief that it is our collective moral obligation to allow seamless, accessible and unhindered use also for those of us with disabilities.

Despite our efforts to make all pages and content on Pappy's Jr fully accessible, some content may not have yet been fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards. This may be a result of not having found or identified the most appropriate technological solution.

Here For You

We are continually seeking out solutions that will bring all areas of the site up to the same level of overall web accessibility. In the meantime, should you experience any difficulty in accessing the Pappy's Jr website, please don't hesitate to contact us. If you do encounter an accessibility issue, please be sure to specify the specific web page and issue in your email, and we will make all reasonable efforts to make that page accessible for you.

Contact Us

If you wish to report an accessibility issue, have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us at pappysjr420@yahoo.com.
