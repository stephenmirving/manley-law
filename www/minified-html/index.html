Manley & Halverstadt, LLC provides full legal service to individuals and businesses throughout the South Carolina coastal region, including Mount Pleasant, Charleston, Moncks Corner, and Summerville. Todd Manley and Matthew Halverstadt offer a mediation and litigation practice with a focus on S.C. family law. The law firm of Manley and & Halverstadt also takes cases ranging from civil and person injury lawsuits to criminal defense.
