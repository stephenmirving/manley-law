Accessibility

Manley & Halverstadt Accessibility Statement

Last updated December, 2023

Manley & Halverstadt, LLC is committed to providing accessibility at our offices as well as on the web. We are actively working to increase the usability of our website for those with visual or motion impairments, and in doing so, adhering to many of the available standards and guidelines for an accessibile web such as the WCAG and the ADA.

Accessibility at Manley & Halverstadt, LLC Offices

Manley & Halverstadt, LLC's office locations are wheelchair accessible and have handicap parking and bathrooms available. If you believe that you may need additional accomodations, please feel free to  contact us  prior to your arrival and we would be happy to ensure your needs for are met. Our Charleston office phone number is (843) 375-1288 and you can reach our Moncks Corner office at (843) 761-7280

Accessibility on www.manleyandhalverstadt.com

Manley & Halverstadt, LLC strives to meet and improve on its compliance with ADA and the WCAG. If you have any issues with using our website due to a disability or issues with assistance devices (e.g. screen readers) please contact our web developer at stephenmichaelirving@gmail.com so that the issue can be promptly resolved.

Disclaimer

Manley & Halverstadt, LLC continues its efforts to constantly improve the accessibility of its site and services in the belief that it is our collective moral obligation to allow seamless, accessible and unhindered use also for those of us with disabilities.

Despite our efforts to make all pages and content on Manley & Halverstadt, LLC fully accessible, some content may not have yet been fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards. This may be a result of not having found or identified the most appropriate technological solution.

Here For You

We are continually seeking out solutions that will bring all areas of the site up to the same level of overall web accessibility. In the meantime, should you experience any difficulty in accessing the Manley & Halverstadt, LLC website, please don't hesitate to contact our offices, or email our web developer directly at stephenmichaelirving@gmail.com. If you do encounter an accessibility issue, please be sure to specify the specific web page and issue in your email, and we will make all reasonable efforts to make that page accessible for you.

Contact Us

If you wish to report an accessibility issue, have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our offices or email us at paralegal@manleylawfirm.com.
